The Tension

Strong activity.
Not yet compounding.

Across campaigns, content, channels, and AI, Sage has real momentum. The challenge is not effort — it is that these initiatives are operating as parallel workstreams rather than a unified growth engine. Every campaign starts fresh. Every market starts from scratch. Performance insights do not feed back into planning. The result: strong investment, limited return.

Fragmentation across lifecycle
Planning, creation, localisation, activation, and measurement are disconnected. No shared campaign object links them.
Manual effort at every stage
Reactive production, heavy agency dependency, limited reusable templates. Teams recreate rather than reuse.
No feedback loop
Weak asset traceability. Limited campaign-to-outcome reporting. Insights not flowing back into planning.
Competitive pressure accelerating
Xero and QuickBooks winning mindshare, especially with accountants. Category is being reshaped by cloud-native UX and AI.
"This is not a lack of activity problem.
It is a connection problem."

The Opportunity

Three pillars to unlock growth

Not separate initiatives — they reinforce each other when connected.

01
Building on the Foundation
Strong capabilities and innovation are already in place. The opportunity is to make them discoverable, reusable, and governed — so teams stop rebuilding and start compounding.
  • Modular content creation and templates
  • Connected DAM with search and reuse
  • Streamlined briefing and approvals
  • Reduced agency dependency
02
Closing the Loop
The biggest unlock. Connect planning, creation, activation, and measurement through shared IDs and end-to-end traceability. Turn disconnected stages into a learning system.
  • Campaign and asset-level attribution
  • Performance data feeding back to planning
  • Unified visibility of what is live
  • Content intelligence, not just content production
03
AI-Enabled Acceleration
AI is the multiplier, not the starting point. When the foundation is connected and the loop is closed, AI can scale what works, reduce manual effort, and accelerate growth.
  • AI-assisted content variation and localisation
  • Automated on-brand review and validation
  • Personalised activation at scale
  • Insight-driven content optimisation
